What is Colour Analysis?

Personal Styling vs Fashion Styling

You know that moment when someone tells you, “You look incredible” even though you’ve only changed your shirt? That’s colour analysis.
When the right colours touch your skin, you glow.
✨ Your face looks fresher.
✨ You need less makeup.
✨ Your confidence soars.
Colour analysis simplifies your life and amplifies your presence.

Why Work With Me?

We’ve all had that moment a stranger says,
“That colour looks stunning on you” and suddenly your confidence doubles. Imagine capturing that feeling on demand, every single day. That’s the gift of true colour analysis. And, that’s the magic I help unlock again and again.
Not through guesswork or Pinterest palettes.
But through science, psychology, and a method adapted to Indian skin tones and contrast levels refined by Master-level training in the UK. Unlike one-size-fits-all quizzes or Western-centric systems, my approach is designed for our complexions, our features, our richness. Because when styled well, Indian skin shines in colours most people wouldn’t dare to touch.

My Approach

I believe colour analysis goes far beyond ‘one bill fits all’ kind of palettes. It’s about understanding how shades truly interact with You, your undertone, your brightness, your contrast, your natural depth. From the moment you sit in my chair, it’s not just about drapes and tones. It’s about You. We test every nuance warmth, brightness, depth, contrast until we land on a palette that:
✔ Makes you look radiant and healthy
✔ Aligns with your lifestyle and aspirations
✔ Helps you shop smarter and dress with ease
It’s not about buying more clothes. It’s about buying right.
